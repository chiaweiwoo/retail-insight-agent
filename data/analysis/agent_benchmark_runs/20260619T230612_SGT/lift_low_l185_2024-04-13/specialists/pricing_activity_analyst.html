3. Caveats

  • Average discount is a global store-level metric; individual item-level mixed shifts (e.g., customers buying higher-priced promoted goods) could influence the total revenue without changing the discount factor.
  • The 14-day history window does not provide a full same-weekday-of-previous-month baseline (returned null). The comparison to Qingming-period Saturday (Apr 06, $41.50) is imperfect due to holiday context.
  • "Activity" category definition is not specified — it may capture temporary price reductions, featured items, or other in-store mechanics. The observed jump in activity sales share could also reflect broader external demand (e.g., local event, weather) rather than a promotional cause.
  • The memo focuses only on store l185 on this single date; attribution of causality would benefit from cross-store or category-level benchmarks.
