This is not a scenario where a price hike or a withdrawal of offers suppressed demand. The decline on 05-09 versus all baselines suggests a **demand-side issue** (e.g., foot traffic drop, local disruption, or post-holiday softness after the Labor Day period) rather than a pricing or promotional driver.

- No deep discounting exists — if management had intended to stimulate volume with aggressive price cuts, that was not the chosen strategy on this day.
- Store-level micro-events (e.g., staffing, weather, local competition, stockouts) are outside the scope of this toolset and could be contributing to the weak sales.
- Average discount factor alone does not reveal *which* categories were discounted — a basket shift toward lower-priced items could also be at play.
