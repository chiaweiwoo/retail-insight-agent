The strong sales move on 2024-05-05 is **likely attributable to pricing/promotion strategy**:

- The analysis covers only one store-day; broader store cluster or chain-level data would help confirm whether this is a local or chain-wide promotional effect.
- Historical same-weekday baseline (4 weeks back, ~$209) may include earlier Labor Day period data, slightly complicating the "normal" comparison.
